Akira ramen was excellent. Don't be afraid it's not spicy at all - in my opinion. Spicy chicken gyoza, a delight... wow! Veggie okonomiyaki, to die for. I can easily recommend this place. Only downside is the food takes a while to be served but it's totally worth the wait.

Judy

Arguably the best ramen in Melbourne! Very charming decor, plus friendly staff, and the music in the store I love, all in all, a very good dining experience. I ordered miso ramen and fried chicken, the fried chicken was sweet chili flavor, fried very good, and the ramen taste was also very good. I recommend the fried chicken here!
